About Voltlab

Voltlab is a smart energy calculator hub that helps you estimate, compare and optimize your solar systems, EV charging, batteries and device power usage.

Our Tools

Solar Calculator

Estimate solar panel capacity, ROI and system size based on your energy consumption.

EV Charging Calculator

Calculate charging time, power needs and costs for electric vehicles.

Battery / EcoFlow Calculator

Determine battery capacity requirements and backup duration for your setup.

Device Power Calculator

Convert between W, kWh and runtime to better understand your device consumption.

Unit Converter

Convert between V, A, W, Wh, kWh and AC/DC values quickly and easily.

Scenario Comparison

Compare 2–3 energy scenarios to choose the most efficient solution.

Saved Scenarios
