# ⚡ VoltLab

Smart energy calculators for solar systems, EV charging, batteries, and electrical devices.

VoltLab helps users make informed energy decisions by providing accurate, physics-based calculations for real-world energy scenarios.

---

## 🚀 What is VoltLab?

VoltLab is a mobile application built with Angular and Capacitor that provides practical energy calculators for:

* Solar panel systems
* Electric vehicles (EV)
* Portable power stations (EcoFlow, Bluetti, etc.)
* Home batteries
* Electrical device consumption
* Unit conversion (kW, kWh, Ah, V, A, W)

The app is designed to simplify complex electrical calculations and make them accessible to everyone.
